COVID-19, caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, is a highly contagious respiratory illness that emerged in late 2019.
The virus spreads primarily through respiratory droplets, and common symptoms include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing.
It has led to a global pandemic, impacting communities worldwide. Preventive measures such as vaccination, wearing masks, and practicing good hygiene are crucial in mitigating its spread.
